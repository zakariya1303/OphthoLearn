Module 2: Mock station

Use this as a script for an OSCE station. Each step can be expanded with prompts you can say out loud.

5–7 minute station

You are the FY1 doctor asked to perform a focused cranial nerve and eye examination on a standardised patient complaining of blurred vision.

Examiner prompt: “Please perform a cranial nerve and eye examination. You do not need to examine other systems. Explain your findings as you go.”
This station assesses cranial nerves II, III and VI. Use the 'I can't believe VAR' mnemonic when performing the steps.

OSCE timer: 07:00

Use this timer to practise under exam conditions. It runs only on your device and does not record any data.

Station focus

  • Introduction, consent and patient safety.
  • Stay structured and explain each step as you go.
  • Finish with a concise summary and next steps.
