Spot Diagnosis Pack

Contents
- images/full/: standardised 1200 x 800 WebP images, padded on a dark background for consistent card display.
- images/thumb/: 480 x 320 WebP thumbnails for case grids.
- data/spot-diagnosis-cases.json: ready-to-use case data.
- data/spot-diagnosis-cases.js: same data exported as a JS constant.
- data/spot-diagnosis-cases.md: human-readable version.

Implementation note
Use the `image`, `thumbnail`, `answer`, and `explanation` fields to populate your flip cards.

Important copyright note
These appear to be textbook/Kanski-derived images. They may be fine for private study, but you should not publish them on a public website unless you have permission or a licence. For a live e-learning resource, use open-licence images, images from your own department with consent/anonymisation, or images you have explicit permission to use.
